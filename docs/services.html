Evidence Synthesis

Transform scattered research into clear, actionable insights through systematic and transparent methodology and reporting.

Systematic Reviews

PRISMA-compliant comprehensive evidence assessment

  • Applications: PhD dissertations, grant applications, clinical guidelines, competitive landscape analysis, regulatory submissions
  • Deliverables: Protocol with PROSPERO registration, full manuscript, PRISMA flow diagram, quality assessment, evidence tables
  • Timeline: 8-12 weeks

Meta-Analysis

Statistical synthesis for precise effect estimates

  • Applications: Publications, research synthesis, demonstrating product superiority, market access dossiers
  • Expertise: Fixed/random effects models, subgroup analysis, meta-regression, publication bias assessment
  • Deliverables: Forest plots, funnel plots, sensitivity analyses, technical reports

Network Meta-Analysis

Compare multiple interventions simultaneously

  • Applications: Treatment rankings for guidelines, indirect comparisons for HTA, competitive positioning
  • Methods: Bayesian and frequentist approaches, consistency assessment, GRADE certainty ratings
  • Deliverables: Network plots, league tables, SUCRA rankings, comprehensive reports

Health Economics and Outcomes Research

Quantify value propositions and optimize healthcare resource allocation with advanced economic modeling.

Cost-Effectiveness Analysis

Full economic evaluation for decision-making

  • Applications: Reimbursement submissions, formulary applications, pricing strategy, policy evaluation
  • Modeling: Markov models, decision trees, discrete event simulation, partitioned survival
  • Outputs: ICERs, cost-effectiveness planes, acceptability curves, value of information analysis

Budget Impact Analysis

Real-world affordability and resource planning

  • Applications: Hospital formulary decisions, payer negotiations, healthcare planning
  • Capabilities: Multi-year projections, scenario analysis, population modeling
  • Deliverables: Excel-based models, executive summaries, presentation decks

Survival Analysis

Time-to-event modeling and extrapolation

  • Applications: Overall survival, progression-free survival, lifetime cost projections
  • Methods: Kaplan-Meier, Cox regression, parametric survival models, flexible spline models
  • Software: R (survival, flexsurv packages)

Decision Curve Analysis

Clinical utility assessment for predictive models

  • Applications: Biomarker evaluation, risk prediction models, diagnostic test evaluation
  • Deliverables: Net benefit curves, clinical impact plots, decision thresholds
