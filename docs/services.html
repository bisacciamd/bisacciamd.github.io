Health Economics and Outcomes Research

Cost-Effectiveness Analysis

Full economic evaluation for decision-making

  • Applications: Reimbursement submissions, formulary applications, pricing strategy, policy evaluation
  • Modeling: Markov models, decision trees, discrete event simulation, partitioned survival
  • Outputs: ICERs, cost-effectiveness planes, acceptability curves, value of information analysis

Budget Impact Analysis

Real-world affordability and resource planning

  • Applications: Hospital formulary decisions, payer negotiations, healthcare planning
  • Capabilities: Multi-year projections, scenario analysis, population modeling
  • Deliverables: Excel-based models, executive summaries, presentation decks

Survival Analysis

Time-to-event modeling and extrapolation

  • Applications: Overall survival, progression-free survival, lifetime cost projections
  • Methods: Kaplan-Meier, Cox regression, parametric survival models, flexible spline models
  • Software: R (survival, flexsurv packages)

Decision Curve Analysis

Clinical utility assessment for predictive models

  • Applications: Biomarker evaluation, risk prediction models, diagnostic test evaluation
  • Deliverables: Net benefit curves, clinical impact plots, decision thresholds
